AI Insights

Powered by your TradeEdge AI backend — scoring, coaching, and pre-trade analysis.

Live Opportunity Scanner

Watches your own open trades (logged from a screenshot, not yet closed) against live MT5 price and flags when price is closing in on or has crossed your SL/TP. No rules, no verdict — just where price sits relative to your own plan. Requires the Live MT5 Feed EA to be attached and pushing price for that symbol.

Auto-journal from MT5 (TradeEdgeAutoJournal.mq5): pulls in trades your MT5 EA has opened/closed automatically -- you still fill in rules/tags/notes yourself afterward.
Clear all trades on the AI backend -- permanently deletes every trade in the AI backend's journal (e.g. starting fresh on a new MT5 account, so the AI Confidence Model and Coach stop mixing in old-account history). This does not touch your local Trade Journal tab -- only the copy the AI backend uses for coaching and ML training. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Coach Deep Dive

My Best Setups

Win rate, R:R, best session/day, and example screenshots per point-of-interest type — ranked purely from your own logged trades, not a fixed list of "good" setups. Each one needs at least a few logged trades before it shows up here.

Your Edge (Best Pattern)

Once "My Best Setups" above looks at one thing at a time (just POI type), this looks at the FULL combination — pair, direction, timeframe, POI type, premium/discount zone, and session all matching at once — and ranks whichever exact combinations you've actually repeated enough times to call a real pattern. Entirely discovered from your own history; nothing here is a fixed "good setup."

Pre-Trade Check

Screenshot-first: take one screenshot of your MT5 chart showing your pending order (Entry/SL/TP) and the OB/FVG/BOS/CHoCH your indicator already marked. The AI reads it and compares it to your own past trades — it never tells you to take or skip a trade, that stays your call.

Chart Analysis Engine

Upload any chart screenshot — the AI reads the pair, timeframe, your pending order (Entry/SL/TP/R:R), and the OB/FVG/BOS/CHoCH your indicator marked, then compares it to your own trade history. No pass/fail rules — just "have I seen this before, and how did it go?"

Setup & Insight

Session & News ● LIVE

Auto-refreshes every 60 seconds — current trading session and any high-impact news due soon, no button needed.

Add your own news event -- always checked alongside the automatic calendar (works even if that calendar isn't configured or is erroring, like a missing/expired API key).

Margin Buffer ● LIVE

A whole-account safety net alongside your per-trade stop losses: how close your account is to XM's margin call (50% margin level) and stop-out (20%) thresholds -- most useful if you have several positions open at once. It only warns -- it never closes anything. Requires the updated MT5 EA (pushes balance/equity/margin each tick).
